Cocomelon (also known as J.J. & Friends or CoComelon) is a YouTube channel and streaming media show that features animated videos of children's songs and nursery rhymes. The channel was created in 2006 and is based in Irvine, California. The channel's videos typically feature catchy tunes and colorful animations that are designed to help children learn through play. Cocomelon has become incredibly popular in recent years, with millions of subscribers and billions of views. The channel has a wide range of content, from traditional nursery rhymes and children's songs to original compositions and educational videos that teach basic concepts like colors, numbers, and letters. Some of the channel's most popular videos include "Bath Song," "Wheels on the Bus," and "ABC Song." The success of Cocomelon has led to a range of merchandise and spinoff shows, including a series on Netflix called "CoComelon Kids." The show has also been translated into multiple languages and is popular in countries all over the world.
